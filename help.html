Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Bastar?

The best time to visit Bastar is from October to March when the weather is pleasant. If you want to see the waterfalls in their full glory, the monsoon season (July to September) is also a good time.

Are permits required to visit tribal areas?

Yes, certain deep tribal areas might require permits. It is advised to travel with a registered local guide who can help arrange necessary permissions and ensure a respectful visit to local communities.

What languages are spoken in Bastar?

Hindi and Chhattisgarhi are widely understood. However, the local tribal population primarily speaks regional languages like Halbi, Gondi, and Bhatri.

Is it safe to travel alone in Bastar?

Major tourist spots in Bastar like Jagdalpur, Chitrakote, and Tirathgarh are safe. However, if you are venturing into remote areas, it is highly recommended to travel in a group or with an authorized local guide.

What should I pack for a Bastar trip?

Pack comfortable cotton clothes, sturdy walking shoes, mosquito repellent, basic first-aid, and cash (as ATMs might be scarce outside major towns). Don't forget your camera!

Safety Tips

  • Always carry identification documents and emergency contact numbers.
  • Inform your hotel about your daily travel plans and expected return time.
  • Respect local customs and tribal traditions during your visit.
  • Stay hydrated and carry basic first aid supplies.
  • Use authorized guides and transport services for remote area visits.
